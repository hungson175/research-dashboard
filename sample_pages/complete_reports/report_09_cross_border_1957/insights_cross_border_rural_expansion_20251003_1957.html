1. Project Nexus: Gateway to 1.7B Users by 2027

BIS's Project Nexus will enable instant cross-border QR payments via single integration, connecting MoMo to 1.7 billion people across ASEAN+3 (live transactions targeted 2027). VietQR-QRIS and PromptPay linkages already live, processing 100M+ transactions/month.

2. Rural Market: 70% Unbanked, High Growth Potential

Rural areas (70% of Vietnam's population) have 30-50% digital payment penetration vs. 60%+ urban. Mobile money reached 6.3M users (72% rural) in 2024. MoMo's 40% non-urban user base shows opportunity to capture 500k+ users via telecom partnerships.

3. UnionPay Partnership: Access to 9B Cards & China Market

UnionPay dominates Vietnam (90%+ ATM/merchant acceptance) and processes 228B global transactions. NAPAS-UnionPay MOU enables QR interoperability, reducing remittance costs by up to 60% vs. SWIFT. VND-CNY settlements grew 27-45% YoY, supporting de-dollarization.

4. VNPay Threat: 22% Growth, Global Transfer Advantage

VNPay reached 33M active users (22% YoY growth) and leads in Tier-3 cross-border transactions. Its Global Transfer service directly competes with MoMo's remittance ambitions. Anticipated Oct 2025 user dip creates window for MoMo to capture 1M users via superior QR network and cashback incentives.

5. Growth Projections: $76B Market by 2030, 35% Revenue Boost

Vietnam's mobile payment market will grow from $47.5B (2025) to $76B (2030) at 9.84% CAGR. MoMo's QR linkages processed 3.5M txns/month (2.5T VND GMV) in 2025. Strategic investment of 3-5T VND in 'MoMo Global QR' can increase rural users by 20%, capture 1M dip users, and boost international revenue by 35%.
