1. Critical October Churn Event

MoMo's active users plummeted from 10.65M (Sep) to 4.7M (Oct 2025), a 56% drop coinciding with VNPay's aggressive conversion strategies. This threatens the 33M total user base and risks falling below the 30M threshold that could impact MoMo's $2B+ valuation.

2. Vietnam E-Wallet Loyalty Crisis

Only 35% of Vietnamese users plan to stick with their current e-wallet (Decision Lab), with users averaging 2-3 wallets. VNPay and ZaloPay dominate alongside MoMo (68% share), while QR code payments surged 846% in early 2024, intensifying competition.

3. AI Personalization ROI

AI-driven retention strategies deliver 25-50% churn reduction, 40% revenue growth, and boost customer lifetime value by 20-50%. WeChat Pay (1.3B users) achieved 300% offer redemption via AI personalization, while Asian fintechs like Grab and Paytm report 15-78% CLV increases.

4. Regulatory Compliance as Competitive Edge

Vietnam's Decree 94/2025 establishes fintech sandbox for Open API/credit scoring (effective July 2025), aligning with PSD3-influenced open banking trends. MoMo's iProov biometric partnership positions it for compliance, while cyber threats surged (8.5M accounts compromised H1 2025).

5. Vietnam's Digital Wallet Growth Trajectory

Vietnam's e-wallet market projects 50M active users by end-2025 (FiinGroup) and $48.6B transaction value by 2025 (up from $14B in 2020). Southeast Asia will reach 75% digital wallet adoption by 2026 (Juniper), with sustainable fintech and loyalty programs driving $30B+ market by 2026.

Strategic Recommendations: MoMo Loyalty AI Hub Implementation

  • Deploy AI-Powered Personalization Engine: Integrate behavioral biometrics (iProov) with predictive analytics to deliver hyper-personalized rewards (targeting 300% redemption increase like WeChat Pay) and reduce churn below 5%.
  • Launch Gamified Loyalty Program: Implement tiered rewards (Bronze → Private Client model like Farfetch) with micro-offsetting for ESG-conscious Gen Z/Millennials, targeting 85% retention of new users and NPS >70.
  • Activate 'Safe Wallet Transfer' Counter-Campaign: Position MoMo as the 'Trusted E-Wallet' via national campaign emphasizing biometric security, compliance with Decree 94/2025, and data protection to counter VNPay's conversion tactics.

  • Target Gen Z & Rural Segments: Use AI to identify churn-prone Gen Z (18-24) and rural/Tier-3 users (limited Sep-Oct data available), offering tailored cashback and financial literacy programs to boost engagement from 60% to 90%+.
