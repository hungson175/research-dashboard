1. BNPL Boom vs. MoMo Share Threat

Vietnam's BNPL market explodes from $1.91B (2024) to $8.51B (2030), but VNPay's 40+ bank partnerships threaten to erode MoMo's 50% share to 40-45%, potentially losing 2M users. MoMo's 10 bank partnerships are insufficient to defend market leadership.

2. Gen Z/Millennial Fintech Surge

Fintech adoption among Gen Z/Millennials in SEA will hit 79% by 2030 (up from 65% in 2024). In Vietnam, 49% already use BNPL (vs. 17% in 2022), with 60% of transactions from under-30s. Quick loans, bank-linked savings, and cashback incentives (e.g., 500K VND) are top demands.

3. Bank Partnership Deficit

MoMo's 10 bank partnerships vs. VNPay's 40+ create a structural disadvantage. Case studies (Grab-Fin, Fundiin-Pharmacity) show partnerships drive 200%+ GMV growth. Expanding to 25 banks via embedded finance models (projected $228B globally by 2028) can unlock 1.5M new users and 30% GMV increase.

4. NPL Reduction & AML Compliance

Vietnam's banking NPL target is <3% by end-2025 (currently 4.8%), while fintech BNPL faces 32% missed repayment rates. Biometric eKYC (99% accuracy, mandated July 2025) and AI credit scoring cut NPL by 50% (per Decision Lab). NHNN Circular 27/2025 requires risk-based AML with mandatory audits.

5. Embedded Finance Fee Revenue

Embedded finance revenue will grow 148% globally ($92B→$228B by 2028), with service fees at 30% of revenue (current MoMo structure). Bank-fintech partnerships enable BNPL (12.6% take rate for Grab), open APIs, and credit scoring. Juniper forecasts Vietnam's mobile money spend at $2.37T by 2029 (51% growth).

6. Decree 94/2025 Sandbox Opportunity

NHNN's Decree 94/2025 (effective July 1, 2025) enables 2-year sandbox testing for credit scoring, open APIs, and P2P lending. This creates a legal framework for MoMo to pilot 'MoMo Finance Secure' with 5-minute eKYC, competitive rates, and reduced compliance risk while innovating on BNPL/quick loans.

Key Sandbox Benefits:
  • ✓ Credit scoring with alternative data (mobile, e-commerce)
  • ✓ Open API data sharing (customer consent-based)
  • ✓ P2P lending platforms (max 2-year loans, VND 100M cap)
  • ✓ Real-time CIC reporting for NPL limits
  • ✓ Regulatory feedback loop for compliance optimization

Strategic Recommendations

  • Expand to 25 bank partnerships by Q4 2026: Prioritize top-tier banks (Vietcombank, BIDV, Techcombank) and fintech-friendly institutions to match VNPay's reach. Target 1.5M new users via co-branded BNPL/quick loan products.
  • Launch 'MoMo Finance Secure' in Decree 94/2025 sandbox: Pilot 5-minute biometric eKYC, AI credit scoring (using mobile/e-commerce data), and competitive 0% interest BNPL for 30-45 days. Aim for NPL <1.2% via real-time CIC integration.
  • Deploy Gen Z/Millennial acquisition campaign: Offer 500K VND cashback for first BNPL/loan transactions, integrate TikTok/Shopee payment partnerships, and create gamified savings products (e.g., Túi Thần Tài 2.0 with tiered rewards).
  • Implement NHNN Circular 27/2025 AML framework: Adopt risk-based scoring (1-5 scale), mandatory biometric verification for high-risk transactions (>VND 10M), and quarterly audits. Partner with VinCSS for FIDO2 passkeys to build trust.
  • Scale embedded finance via open APIs: Enable bank data sharing (with consent) to power personalized loan rates, cross-sell insurance/wealth products, and create a unified 'MoMo Super Wallet' ecosystem. Target 30% financial GMV increase by 2027.
  • Reduce NPL to <1.5% via AI-powered tools: Deploy AI fraud detection (cutting losses by 50% per TPBank case study), real-time transaction monitoring, and behavioral analytics. Offer financial literacy programs to 73.7% of users demanding education.
  • Position as 'Digital Financial Security' leader: Launch marketing campaign highlighting MoMo's compliance advantage (biometric eKYC, NHNN sandbox approval, bank-grade security) to counter VNPay's Finance Hub and regain 50%+ BNPL market share by 2028.
