Vietnam E-Wallet Market: Competitive Intelligence 2025

Vietnam's digital payment market is projected to reach $7.19B in 2025 (26.6% CAGR), but MoMo faces intensifying threats from banks' domestic prepaid cards and super-app integrations by ZaloPay/VNPAY. Despite holding 68% market share, MoMo's active users declined to 14.4M in 2025 (from 31M claims), signaling urgent need for defensive strategies in BNPL, cross-border QR, and financial services to protect its $971B transaction volume.

Competing E-Wallets

1. Market Share Under Pressure

MoMo's claimed 68% share masks declining active users (14.4M vs 31M claimed). Bank apps now hold 34% penetration with 26% primary usage, eroding MoMo's dominance despite 47.3% projected share by 2030.

2. Banks Launch Prepaid Card Offensive

Vietcombank, BIDV, and Sacombank are launching NAPAS-backed domestic prepaid cards with lower fees, directly targeting MoMo's mobile payment core. VietQR integration enables 106.7% QR transaction surge, bypassing e-wallets.

3. Gen Z Switching for Fees & Integrations

65% of users cite promotions as key driver; 88% of Gen Z prefer cashless. MoMo leads with 86% active rate, but competitors gain via super-app integrations (ZaloPay +60% tx volume Q1 2025) and lower fees.

4. Consolidation & BNPL Growth

From 40+ e-wallets to super-apps via mergers (Vimo-mPOS). BNPL market explodes to $2.61B (36.5% growth 2025), offering MoMo new revenue stream to offset payment margin pressure.

5. Internal Product Health Check

MoMo's 2025 transaction volume reached VND 970.9T ($40.5B). Moni chatbot users peaked at 301K in Sept but plummeted to 28K in Oct. Group savings (P2P) grew to VND 44T by Sept, showing financial services traction.

6. Regulatory Shift & Cross-Border Race

SBV Circular 40/2024 (July 2025) recognizes e-wallets as payment method, enabling wallet-to-wallet transfers. ZaloPay leads cross-border QR (China, Korea, Singapore), while NAPAS-Laos link (Jan 2025) opens regional expansion.

Strategic Recommendations for MoMo

  • Launch BNPL Aggressively: Capture $2.61B market (36.5% CAGR) to offset payment margin erosion; partner with e-commerce platforms like Shopee/Lazada for embedded financing.
  • Expand Cross-Border QR: Counter ZaloPay's international lead by integrating VietQR with regional networks (China, ASEAN); target $19.56B remittance market via Western Union partnership.
  • Strengthen Bank Partnerships: Co-create domestic prepaid cards with NAPAS to leverage bank trust while maintaining MoMo's user experience; avoid being bypassed by bank-fintech alliances.
  • Accelerate Super-App Strategy: Integrate micro-investments (Túi Thần Tài scale-up), insurance, and stock trading to increase user stickiness; current financial services show traction (VND 44T group savings).
  • Fix Moni Chatbot Engagement: Address 90% drop-off (301K→28K Oct) via AI personalization (leverage Pique acquisition); use for financial literacy and upselling BNPL/savings products.
  • Target Rural/Tier 2-3 Cities: Banks lag in rural penetration (30% intermittent access); expand mobile money pilot (extended to Dec 2025) to capture 50M unbanked adults before consolidation.
  • Enhance Security & Trust: Combat 13,900 annual fraud cases with advanced biometrics/tokenization; emphasize transparency to counter bank trust advantage (86.97% hold bank accounts vs e-wallet skepticism).
